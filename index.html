About Our School & Details

Founded with a vision to nurture young minds, Princes Padmaraje Girls High School stands as a beacon of learning and empowerment. We provide a safe, competitive, and supportive environment designed explicitly for girls to flourish academically and socially.


Establishment: Registered Institution providing state-board aligned standard curricula with modern modern pedagogical approaches.

Quick Specifications

  • Type: Girls Only High School
  • Medium: English & Regional Languages
  • Facilities: Science Labs, Smart Classrooms, Library, Playground

Our Classes & Curriculum

We provide comprehensive educational programs split across various age-appropriate levels:

Primary Section

Grades 1 to 5. Focusing on fundamental skills, creativity, and structural learning habits.

Secondary Section

Grades 6 to 10. Focused preparation for board examinations alongside robust extracurricular exposure.

Admission Process

Admissions for the upcoming academic session are currently open. Parents can secure enrollment by following these simple steps:

  1. Pick up the Application Form from the administration desk.
  2. Submit required documentation (Birth Certificate, Previous Report Cards, Transfer Certificate).
  3. Attend the interaction/interview round.

School Bus Transport Facility

We operate a secure fleet of school buses covering all major routes across the city to ensure hassle-free transit for our students.

  • GPS Tracking enabled vehicles.
  • Trained female attendants present on every route.
  • Strict adherence to safety guidelines and speed limits.

School Code of Conduct & Rules

To maintain decorum and a healthy learning environment, students are expected to adhere to the following regulations:

  • Attendance: Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory to appear for term examinations.
  • Uniform: Students must arrive in neat, prescribed school uniform daily.
  • Discipline: Mobile phones and other electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited on campus premises.
